Putting

Around 40-50% of total shots are putts. A good day on the greens is the most important factor in shooting a low score.

Chipping

Improving your chipping will take pressure off other parts of your game. If you're not afraid to miss the greens, you'll probably hit more of them.

Bunker shots

Playing from the sand is daunting. Put the time in on the practice range and sand-saves will become second nature.
